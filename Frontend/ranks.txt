Lowest to Highest Ranks

1. Novice – Just getting started.
2. Apprentice – Building a foundation.
3. Challenger – Actively engaging in quizzes.
4. Analyst – Developing critical thinking.
5. Scholar – Strong subject understanding.
6. Mastermind – Excelling in multiple topics.
7. Strategist – Applying knowledge with precision.
8. Expert – Consistently achieving high scores.
9. Elite – Among the top performers.
10. Grandmaster – The pinnacle of knowledge.
